Why ?

  • Ideal for Beginners: Offers a straightforward approach with uncomplicated building blocks suitable for creating any design system.
  • Streamlined HTML Semantics: Boasts a clean and intuitive HTML structure, with inherent support for mobile devices, grid layouts, and printing.
  • Compatibility with Code-Generated Content: Designed to seamlessly integrate with code-generated content in popular frameworks like React, Vue, and Angular
  • Harmonious Styling Integration: Ensures safety when including it in your styles and plays well with other frameworks.
  • Night Mode Support: Comes equipped with support for night mode, enhancing the user experience in low-light conditions."
